üîç Understanding the Challenge

The challenge title "SSS" immediately provides a crucial hint - it stands for Shamir's Secret Sharing algorithm. This cryptographic scheme allows a secret to be divided into multiple shares, where a minimum threshold of shares is required to reconstruct the original secret.

üîê Shamir's Secret Sharing Basics:

  • Polynomial-based: Uses polynomial interpolation over finite fields
  • Threshold scheme: Requires k out of n shares to reconstruct the secret
  • Information-theoretic security: Fewer than k shares reveal nothing about the secret
  • Perfect reconstruction: Exactly k shares perfectly reconstruct the original

üí° Key Insight: The challenge description mentions "Shamir will be proud" and provides exactly 3 shares (P1, P2, P3), suggesting we need all three to reconstruct the secret containing the flag.
